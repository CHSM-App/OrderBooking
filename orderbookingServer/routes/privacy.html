Section 01

Overview

Retail Pulse is a field sales and order booking management application developed and operated by Vengurla Tech Pvt. Ltd., a company registered in India.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use the Retail Pulse mobile app, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Retail Pulse, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

This policy applies to all users of Retail Pulse — including company administrators and field sales employees — operating within India.
Section 02

Who Uses Retail Pulse

Retail Pulse has two types of users within each company account, each with different roles and data access:

Admin (One per Company)
Registers the company, manages employees and products, and views field activity reports including location history
Employees (Field Sales)
Check in/out daily, visit shops, take orders, and have their location recorded at key activity points throughout the day
Section 03

Data We Collect

We collect different data depending on your role in the app. All data collected is strictly necessary for the app to function.

Admin — Company Registration Data

  • Company name, owner name, mobile number, email address
  • Company address and GSTIN number

Admin — Employee Data (entered by admin)

  • Employee name, phone number, and address
  • Identity proof document (e.g. Aadhaar, PAN) uploaded during employee onboarding

Admin — Product & Business Data

  • Product names, units, and pricing added by the admin

Employee — Activity & Location Data

  • GPS location at check-in (start of workday)
  • GPS location when punching into a shop visit
  • GPS location at check-out (end of workday)
  • Shop details added manually by employee (no automatic location on shop creation)
  • Orders taken at each shop during the day
  • Daily attendance records

Automatically Collected

  • Device model, OS version, and app version (via analytics)
  • App usage patterns such as screen views and crash reports
Section 04

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve the Retail Pulse platform. Here is how data is used for each role:

For Admins

  • To create and manage the company account and product catalogue
  • To register and manage employee profiles and access
  • To view employee attendance records
  • To view the full daily route of each employee — from check-in location, through each shop visited, to checkout location
  • To review and manage orders taken by employees

For Employees

  • To record and verify the start and end of each working day via check-in and check-out
  • To log shop visits and capture GPS location when punching into a shop
  • To place and manage customer orders from visited shops
  • To maintain an accurate and auditable daily activity record

We do not sell your personal data to any third party, we do not use it for advertising purposes, and we do not send any push notifications or marketing communications.

Section 05

Location Tracking

Location access is a core feature of Retail Pulse for employee users. The app captures GPS coordinates at the following specific moments:

  • Check-In — When an employee starts their workday, their location is recorded
  • Shop Punch-In — When an employee taps on a saved shop to begin a visit, their current GPS location is captured
  • Check-Out — When an employee ends their workday, their location is recorded
Important: Location is captured only at these three specific action points. The app does not continuously track or record your GPS position in the background throughout the day.

Admins can view the captured location data of their employees to monitor field activity, verify visits, and review daily routes. This location data is visible only to the admin of the employee's own company.

Employees are informed at the time of onboarding that their location will be captured at check-in, shop punch-in, and check-out. By using the app, employees acknowledge and consent to this location capture.

Location permission can be revoked from device settings at any time, but doing so will prevent check-in, punch-in, and check-out from functioning correctly.

Section 06

Employee Monitoring & Transparency

Retail Pulse includes features that allow admins to monitor field employee activity. This is a core business function of the app. The following monitoring features are present:

  • View employee check-in and check-out times and locations
  • View the list of shops visited by an employee and the punch-in location for each visit
  • View orders placed by each employee
  • View attendance records
All monitoring is limited to work-related activities within the app. Employees should be aware that their activity data including location snapshots is visible to their company admin.

Employees are encouraged to speak with their admin if they have questions about how their activity data is stored or used within their organisation.

Section 07

Identity Documents

When an admin adds a new employee to Retail Pulse, an identity proof document (such as an Aadhaar card, PAN card, or similar government-issued ID) may be uploaded as part of the employee profile.

  • This document is stored securely and is only accessible to the company admin
  • It is used solely for internal employee verification purposes
  • It is not shared with any third party under any circumstances
  • Employees may request to view or delete their identity document by contacting their admin or reaching us directly
Identity documents are sensitive data. We handle them with strict access controls and they are never used for any purpose other than employee onboarding verification within the company.
Section 08

Third-Party Services

Retail Pulse uses the following third-party services that may collect certain data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google Maps SDK — Used to render interactive maps within the app, allowing admins to visualise employee check-in, shop punch-in, and check-out locations on a map. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps Directions API — Used to display route paths between the location points recorded during an employee's working day, giving admins a clear view of the daily field route. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

We use only the two Google Maps services listed above. We do not use any analytics, advertising, or crash reporting SDKs. No personally identifiable information is shared with any third party beyond what is technically required for the Google Maps features to function.

Section 09

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Sensitive files such as identity documents are stored with restricted access
  • Access to production systems is limited to authorised Vengurla Tech personnel only
  • We retain your data for as long as your account is active, or as required by applicable Indian law
  • Upon account deletion request, your personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days

While we take data security seriously, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage all users to keep their login credentials confidential and report any suspected unauthorised access immediately.

Section 10

Your Rights

As a user of Retail Pulse, you have the following rights regarding your personal data under applicable Indian law (including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023):

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data, including identity documents (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Withdrawal of Consent — Withdraw consent to location data capture or other processing at any time
  • Grievance Redressal — Raise a complaint if you believe your data has been mishandled

Employees whose data is entered by their admin (such as name, phone, ID proof) may contact us directly to exercise these rights. To exercise any of these rights, please use the contact details in the section below.

Section 11

Children's Privacy

If you believe a minor has been registered on the platform, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove such information promptly.
